Prompt A: Medium A. create an artwork using watercolor painting that emphasize the texture, line, contrast, movement and unity of an peaceful landscape with flowing river, trees, and distant mountains at sunrise. use soft watercolor brushes, water and water color paper to apply the wet on wet and layering techniques. ALso use transparent washes to create soft gradients in the sky, allowing colors to naturally bleed into each other. 

Prompt B: Medium B. create an artwork using linocut printmaking  that emphasize the texture, line, contrast, movement and unity of an peaceful landscape with flowing river, trees, and distant mountains at sunrise. Use linoleum carving tools such as gouges and cutters to carve into a linoleum block then apply printing ink with a brayer and transfer the image onto paper using a relief printing process. Focus on bold carved lines and strong black-and-white contrast to define the river, trees, and mountains. Use curved carved lines to guide the movement of the flowing river through the composition.
