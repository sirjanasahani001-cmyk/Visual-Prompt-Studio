Title of the artwork
Rhythm of the block
Medium- The art was made in a street art style, spray paint being the main medium which results in the use of the bold colors and soft shading as well as rough surface texture which is typical in murals you find in urban environment. 
Meaning- The artwork is basically showing the beauty and everday life of urban neighbourhood. It shows the community, culture, resilience and also protests against stereotypes and raises the issues like gentrification, social inequality and the importance of preserving neighbourhood identity. 
Subject Matter- The subject matter includes tall city apartment buildings, a corner store, a basketball court, silhouettes of people (such as children playing and someone sitting outside), graffiti, an elevated train passing overhead, a sunset sky. All of these element together represent the rhythm and culture of urban life.
Art style: The art style of this artwork is representational art. It shows real place and real object which is easily recognizable like buildings, trains, people, store, and basket ball court. Even though it is using bold colors and rough surface texture, it still represent the real- world scenes.
